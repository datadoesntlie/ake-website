About Me

Data Analyst turning complex processes into simple solutions

Transforming Business Operations Through Data-Driven Solutions

I specialize in project and process management, helping clients design and implement efficient workflows that align with their business goals. For the last year, I have been a full-time consultant for Blue, a versatile SaaS platform that clients use not only for project management but also as their CRM and database.

My expertise lies in helping clients implement Blue into their operations and connecting it with other platforms through APIs using tools like Zapier, Make.com, Pabbly, Albato, and n8n. I recently built a custom node for n8n to make Blue available as a native application.

I transform operational data into actionable dashboards and KPIs, enabling project teams to track progress, identify bottlenecks, and make informed, data-driven decisions. What excites me about consulting is working with clients across diverse industries - from logistics in Canada and the US, transport in the UK, cannabis companies in Australia, accounting firms in Belgium, to private schools in Spain.
