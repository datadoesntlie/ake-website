About Me

Data Analyst turning complex processes into simple solutions

Transforming Business Operations Through Data-Driven Solutions

I’ve always had an eye for optimizing processes—identifying bottlenecks and reducing manual work wherever possible. This led me to a career in Engineering Management and along the way, I discovered the beauty of data and how it highlight processes in need of optimization. Later, I landed various data analysis roles, where I learned to program, and automated processes reducing my working hours by 80%. Now, I specialize in process management, helping clients design and implement efficient workflows that align with their business goals.

I started using Blue in 2023 tired of getting lost in emails and the usual task management tools that nobody use because are too complicated. I embraced Blue so much that shared with my collegues, and in 2024 I started consulting for Blue, helping clients implement Blue into their operations and connecting it with other platforms through APIs using tools like Zapier, Make and n8n.

Whatever process you want to optimize and whichever industry you’re in, there’s always room for improvement. Together, we can design processes tailored to your specific needs and what makes your business unique. I’ve worked with clients around the world and the outcome is always the same: less manual work and greater efficiency.
