The Challenge

A handyman company serving schools and government offices across the United States was struggling to coordinate contractors across multiple simultaneous projects. The manager was overwhelmed trying to track job progress, contractor availability, and client communications through a fragmented system of emails and phone calls. Previous attempts to implement project management solutions like Asana and other platforms had failed due to poor adoption and lack of integration with their existing workflows.

No contractor availability visibility

Disconnected client communication

Manual quote and invoice creation

The Solution

Conducted comprehensive process mapping and workflow optimization using Blue Project Management, creating a unified system for managing field service operations through systematic discovery and end-to-end process documentation.

Process Discovery & Mapping

  • Two 1-hour discovery sessions
  • End-to-end process documentation
  • Pain point analysis
  • Identified automation opportunities

Systematic Workflow Design

  1. 1 Work Request Intake
  2. 2 Contractor Assignment
  3. 3 Initial Inspection
  4. 4 Proposal Generation
  5. 5 Work Execution
  6. 6 Client Communication
  7. 7 Invoicing & Feedback

Role-Based Access Control

  • Manager dashboard with complete visibility
  • Contractor mobile access for field updates
  • Client portal access for real-time visibility
  • Customized permissions for all stakeholders

Process Automation

  • Automated client communications for status updates
  • Template-based proposals for repeat services
  • Streamlined invoicing process automation
  • Completion notifications and feedback surveys
